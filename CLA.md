preserve the ability to offer Console under the [GNU AGPL-3.0](LICENSE) **and**,
where needed, under a separate commercial license, MooseQuest LLC ("MooseQuest")
asks every contributor to agree to the terms below. This protects you, the
project, and its users, and it lets the project be relicensed or dual-licensed
without having to track down every past contributor.

This Agreement is between you (the "Contributor", "You") and MooseQuest. It
covers any past and future Contribution You submit to the project.

## 1. Definitions

- **"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship — code, documentation,
  configuration, or other material — that You intentionally submit to MooseQuest
  for inclusion in, or documentation of, Console (a "Submission"), in any form and
  through any medium (e.g. a pull request, patch, or issue attachment), excluding
  anything You conspicuously mark "Not a Contribution."
- **"Submit"** means any form of electronic, verbal, or written communication sent
  to MooseQuest or its representatives, including the project's repositories and
  issue trackers.

You acknowledge that MooseQuest is **not obligated** to use or incorporate Your
Contributions. The decision to include any Contribution is at MooseQuest's sole
discretion.

## 7. Corporate contributions

If You are contributing on behalf of a company, the company should execute a
**Corporate CLA** designating which individuals are authorized to contribute on
its behalf. Contact MooseQuest to arrange a Corporate CLA. (A corporate template
will be added once the individual CLA clears legal review.)

## 8. How to sign

Until an automated CLA tool (e.g. a CLA-assistant bot on pull requests) is
configured, signify Your agreement by adding a line to your first pull request:

```
I have read the CLA document and I hereby sign the CLA.
```

and adding your name and the date to `CONTRIBUTORS.md` in that PR. MooseQuest
will record acceptance. (We expect to replace this manual step with a CLA bot.)
